\section*{Data Availability}
    
The time-series data (1972–2023) used in this study, including the Gini index, trade openness, and fiscal policy indicators, were compiled from the World Bank’s World Development Indicators and the Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED). All datasets and the STATA code used for the VAR(3) estimation, unit root tests, and IRF generation are available for replication at: \href{https://github.com/trmuba2502/Time-Series-Econometrics/tree/main/Final%20Project}{GitHub: trmuba2502/Time-Series-Econometrics}
